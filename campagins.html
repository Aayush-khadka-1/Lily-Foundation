Campaigns

What we think, wear and eat matters. That's why, at Species Unite, we share solutions to some of the world's most pressing issues caused by our reliance on animal products. We currently have four campaigns running annually, designed to help change the world for animals.

7-Day Challenge: What We Think, Wear And Eat Matters.

New to 2022, our 7-day challenge is for animal-lovers who are ready to take the next step to living in line with their animal-loving values. Tested and approved by our plant-based foodie and fashion-loving founder, Elizabeth Novogratz, you'll discover everything you need to become a well-fed, stylish animal-lover.

30-Day Vegan Challenge

Our 30-day vegan challenge sees people ditch the animal products in favor of plant-based foods for an entire month. With daily emails packed full of delicious recipes, top tips, and tons of inspiration, you'll experience how tasty and exciting plant-powered foods can be, all while helping to save animals and reduce your environmental impact.

Vegan Nights: Host A Plant-Based Dinner

Our vegan nights' campaign encourages family or friends to come together, share delicious food, and foster meaningful discussions around the benefits of plant-based eating. To help those hosting a plant-based dinner, Species Unite offers a free downloadable host guide, containing recipes, movie recommendations, activity suggestions, and more.
